Steganography Research

My work in steganography was based around establishing whether a least significant bit approach with a capacity of 50% could be acceptible for steganography which only needed to be resistant to a visual attack when compared to an approach such as the Bit Plane Complexity Segmentation algorithm. This work resulted in a publications entitled 'Comparison of high capacity steganography techniques', details of which can be found on my University Profile here.
